Snip is a comprehensive URL shortening platform that helps you create, manage, and track shortened URLs with advanced features like custom domains, QR codes, analytics, and more.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="link" title="URL Shortening">
    Create shortened URLs with custom codes, expiration dates, and password protection
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="chart-line" title="Advanced Analytics">
    Track clicks, geographic data, devices, referrers, and more
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="qrcode" title="QR Code Generation">

  </Accordion>

  </Accordion>

## Use Cases

- **Marketing Campaigns**: Track campaign performance with UTM parameters
- **Social Media**: Share clean, branded links on social platforms
- **Email Marketing**: Monitor email click-through rates
- **Print Materials**: Generate QR codes for offline marketing
- **API Integration**: Integrate URL shortening into your applications
- **Team Collaboration**: Manage links across your organization
